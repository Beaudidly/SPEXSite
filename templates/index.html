RIT Space Exploration

A Student-faculty research group at Rochester Institute of Technology

CubeSat

NASA's CubeSat Launch Initiative grants undergrad groups like SPEX to be able to work on and develop picosatellites. SPEX is currently in the design and planning phase of our mission.

High Altitude Balloon

HAB allows for the unique opportunity of testing CubeSat technologies in near space-like environments. With launches planned for every semester, HAB is a great way for students to get involved.

SPEXCast

A discussion podcast on the science and technology of space exploration, featuring interviews with experts and industry professionals within the space community.
