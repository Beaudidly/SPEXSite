Cold Gad Propulsion Analysis and Design

The Cold Gas Propulsion Analysis and Design (CGPAD) team concerns the design, fabrication, and experimentation of a cold gas thruster. The projectâ€™s main goal is to provide insight and experience into the design of a supersonic nozzle for small propulsion systems. Secondary goals will include the understanding in the design of high-pressure flow systems, including the necessary precautions and procedures to ensure safe usage.


Our Propulsion System

Our propulsion system includes a regular, a solenoid, a tank, a nozzle, piping and interfaces all connected in series. The test stand includes a t-slot frame made entirely of aluminum. There is also an connected Arduino which will record the data from the load cell.
